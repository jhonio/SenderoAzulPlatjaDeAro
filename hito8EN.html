Sa Conca Blue Flag Beach


This beach is protected by the Punta de Sa Conca in the southern part, and by the Punta de Pinell or Punta Prima in the northern part. Sa Conca beach consists of two coves, differentiated and linked by a small promontory, situated in the central part of the beach, called the islet of Sa Conca. This is a very little beach, scarcely developed and which, even today, retains the charm that has made the Costa Brava famous. It has been awarded the Blue Flag.
